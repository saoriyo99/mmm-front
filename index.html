Herman Miller Aeron Office Chair Review

The Herman Miller Aeron office chair is reviewed on GearLab. It is considered a top-tier office chair with a mesh back that performed well in tests, earning a high score. The chair is noted for being comfortable, durable, and easy to assemble, but comes at a premium price. The adjustability features are good, although it lacks certain adjustments compared to other options. The chair is praised for its ventilation and construction quality, receiving high marks for durability. Overall, the Herman Miller Aeron is recommended for users who prioritize comfort and quality, despite the higher cost.
